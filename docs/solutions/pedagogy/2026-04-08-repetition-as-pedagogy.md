## Context

The cross-module review flagged Act IV of vibecoding (8 tool slides) as CRITICAL because it duplicated Tiago's 42-page `01_tools_ecosystem.pdf`. The trainer chose to keep the overlap.

## Principle

The repetition of content within a document or between documents is a useful pedagogical technique. Participants hear the same concepts (autonomy spectrum, tools, VibeEngineering principles) in different contexts, which reinforces retention.

## Application in reviews

During a cross-module coherence review, content overlaps between trainers or modules must be:
- **Signaled** to the trainer (flagged as potential duplication)
- **Classified as SKIPPED (pedagogical choice)**, not CRITICAL
- **Left to the trainer** to adjust pacing in the live session

## Applies when

Any multi-trainer, multi-module training where theoretical and practical sessions cover related topics. The trainer decides the appropriate level of repetition based on the audience and timing.
